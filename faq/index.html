FAQ

We believe that the Internet Technology has been massively transforming lives of people on this planet. Be it learning, working, solving real-life challenges etc. With abundance of resources available online, people need access to low-cost natural space to upskill and to be a part of this internet revolution, also influence the economy and make a living out of it. HackerFarm serves itself as a physical space and it’s functional because of people. The modular, temporary basic setups like living building, kitchen, bathroom and hi-speed net connection are arranged to make sure that we stay connected with the global online sphere.

1. WHAT EXACTLY IS HACKERFARM

HackerFarm is an experimental temporary community of people.. a home for those who are seeking a self-reliant life, awy from city bustle. It happens on a rural plot, a farm where participants are invited to come together and engage in exploration of their creativity, collaborate with like-minded peers, learn and share from other communities as well. People have to take care of their own needs. Basic infrastructure is setup like kitchen, bathroom etc while people live in tents for the entire duration.

2. HOW SHOULD I APPLY FOR HACKERFARM?

We are currently offering camping space to people and would like them to choose from one of the camps mentioned below. One can select self/group study codecamp or JavaScript Bootcamp or join under Digital Nomad Camp.

  • Apply for Group Study Code Camps (Web Development, Python, ChatBots, A.I., Data Science)
  • Apply for JavaScript Bootcamp (To Be Announced) Register your interest here

4. DO I NEED TO PAY CONTRIBUTE ANYTHING?

We ask people to cover their own living expenses which comes between ~7k-10k INR/month for stay, food, electricity & internet.

5. WILL I GET TRAINING, JOB ASSISTANCE, TEACHERS? IS IT A SCHOOL?

No. HF is just a physical space enabler. It is not a school, training institute or placement center.

We would like people to learn and work as much as they can on their own, build portfolio projects. We might be able to assist them in placement coaching upto some extent, to get into startups or start as freelance developer, but we DO NOT PROMISE or guarantee jobs.
