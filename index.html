Live in Nature. Do Yoga. Learn to Code

We're looking for young, passionate and motivated people to come live together on a farm near Bangalore and study how to build web & mobile apps.

It is an open co-learning environment set up to help people become technology professionals, and pioneer a simple sustainable way of living in nature. Participants live in tents on pallet racks. We do yoga weekday mornings, and play volley ball or ping-pong many afternoons, share food and enjoy a relaxed way of being. Mentor support is available online and office-hours twice a week. People from the startup community also visit. We host sessions and workshops around wide range of topics such as technology, electric unicycles, solar & cyber security. Study participants who demonstrate their technical ability will be invited to showcase their portfolio to startup community where they may be offered an internship.
