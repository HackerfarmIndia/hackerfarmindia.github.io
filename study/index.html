STUDY CAMPS

There is more to life than programming and much in technology than just online classes.

Study Camp is the core camp at HackerFarm and it exists to prepare people to learn skills & work in the technology industry. People take online classes, do prep work for Coding Bootcamps, or go throuh remote coding bootcamp curriculum. Its an open co-learning environment.

First, we do yoga.. then we do the things.

→STUDY GROUPS WITH PEERS

1. Web Development using FreeCodeCamp

2. Mobile App Development

3. Data Science on Kaggle

4. Blockchain/Ethereum

5. Python

6. Prep for a Coding Bootcamp Launch School, Lambda School etc.

We expect participants to set their own goals, form a group and go through dedicated online curriculum.

  • Sessions/Workshops to accelerate the learning
  • Pair Programming and Friday Code Review
  • Mentorship & Performance Tracking

We use tools like

  • Asana for Daily Task Management
  • Google Forms for tracking the Weekly Progress
  • Slack & Web Whatsapp for communication

Study participants who can demonstrate their technical ability at the end of program, will be invited to showcase their portfolio to well known startups community where they may be offered an internship or a job.

People should have passion for programming and can demonstrate by taking some online classes before applying. Do mail us your public profile link to get hackerfarm invitation sooner.
