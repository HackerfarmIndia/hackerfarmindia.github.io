1. FULL STACK WEB DEVELOPMENT BOOTCAMP

Basic understanding of HTML, CSS & some JavaScript or other programming experience is required to attend this camp

You will learn:

  • CommandLine & Git
  • HTML5 & CSS3
  • JavaScript & DOM
  • FrontEnd Library: React
  • Server-side Programming: Node Js
  • Data Persistence: NoSql & Firebase

Participants will go through level-wise learning and assessments for Web-Development Camp.

We set goals for participants, form a group and then go through dedicated curriculum. Program commitment is 50 hours/week.

  • Sessions/Workshops to accelerate the learning
  • Pair Programming and Weekly Code Review
  • Mentorship, Office Hours & Performance Tracking

We use tools like

  • HackerRank everyday to sharpen problem-solving skills
  • GitHub to push learning and project related work.
  • Asana for Daily Task Management
  • Google Forms for tracking Weekly Progress
  • Slack & Web Whatsapp for communication

Daily/weekly updates by learners are tweeted personally and RTed at HackerFarm India on Twitter and also published on personal/medium/dev blogs. Participants should blog regulary to maintain their tech diary.

Study participants who demonstrate their technical ability at the end of program, will be invited to showcase their portfolio to well known startups community where they may be offered an internship or a job.

People should have passion for programming and can demonstrate by taking some online classes before applying. Do mail us your public profile link to get hackerfarm invitation sooner.
